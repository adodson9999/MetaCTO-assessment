Job Description, 10a Labs AI Red Teamer, Cyber
AI Red Teamer, Cyber
About 10a Labs: 10a Labs is the safety and threat-intelligence layer trusted by frontier AI labs, AI unicorns,
Fortune 10 companies, and leading global technology platforms. Our adversarial red teaming, model evaluations,
and intelligence collection enable engineering, safety, and security teams to stay ahead of evolving threats and
deploy AI systems safely.
About the role: We are seeking an AI Red Teamer with deep cybersecurity expertise to help evaluate the security
and resilience of advanced AI systems and applications. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys offensive
security, creative problem-solving, and thinking like an adversary.
In this role, you will: Design and execute red team assessments against AI systems, applications, and supporting
infrastructure. Translate ambiguous objectives into structured testing plans and attack strategies. Develop
adversarial testing methodologies to evaluate system security, robustness, and resilience. Craft prompts, attack
chains, and adversarial inputs to uncover unintended or unsafe system behavior. Identify vulnerabilities, security
risks, and failure modes through testing and analysis. Analyze system outputs and behaviors to assess security
and safety risks. Document findings, communicate risk, and provide actionable recommendations. Collaborate
with engineers, researchers, and subject-matter experts to improve system security. Continuously refine testing
methodologies based on emerging threats and attacker techniques.
Required Qualifications: 2 to 5 years of relevant professional experience, with a track record of taking ownership
of complex projects and driving them to completion. Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Cyber Security,
or a related field (or equivalent practical experience). Strong understanding of cybersecurity principles, attack
methodologies, and common vulnerability classes. Experience with security tools such as Splunk, Wireshark,
Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nmap, or similar platforms. Comfortable working in Linux and terminal-based
environments. Experience scripting or automating workflows using Python, Bash, or similar languages.
Familiarity with SIEM platforms and security monitoring concepts. Ability to think creatively from an adversarial
perspective and investigate complex technical problems. Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
Preferred Qualifications: CompTIA Security+ certification and/or Google Cybersecurity Certificate. Experience
testing AI, machine learning, or large language model applications. Familiarity with prompt injection, jailbreak
techniques, or other AI attack methodologies. Offensive security certifications such as PNPT, OSCP, CySA+,
GCIH, or GPEN. Experience supporting government, defense, intelligence, or critical infrastructure environments.
Compensation & Benefits: Salary Range $100K to $120K depending on experience and location.
Performance-based annual bonus. Support for conferences, continuing education, or leadership training. Fully
remote, U.S.-based. Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage. Generous PTO and paid holiday
schedule. 401(k) plan.
