Job Description, Blackthorn.io Sr. QA Engineer
Sr. QA Engineer
Description: As a Senior QA Engineer at Blackthorn, you will be part of an elite team, testing solutions that solve
our most critical business priorities. You will work closely with various departments to understand our customers'
most pressing needs and provide technical expertise to optimize our testing, prevent issues, and ensure we
deliver high quality products. This role requires advanced skills in test automation, performance testing, and
mentoring junior team members. Base Salary: $5,750 to $7,000 USD per month.
What you'll do: Create detailed, comprehensive, and well-structured test plans and test cases. Review tickets
(bugs, enhancements, new features) and other technical documents to engage in each phase of the software
development lifecycle. Create, run, repair manual and automated tests using frameworks like Playwright. Plan
and build automated testing frameworks that integrate with development workflows, determining what to
automate (regression tests, API endpoints, critical user flows) and when to run which tests. Estimate, prioritize,
plan, coordinate, and perform quality testing activities. Collaborate closely with cross-functional scrum teams and
across the larger company. Demonstrate best practices including meaningful test coverage and maintainability.
Mentor junior QA team members and advocate for quality standards. Design and maintain test data strategies,
including creating hundreds of thousands of Salesforce records for performance testing, managing data versions,
and ensuring clean test environments. Design and execute performance testing strategies to identify system
bottlenecks, load limits, and optimization opportunities. Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines (GitHub
Actions, Cumulus CI, MetaDeploy) to provide fast feedback and act as deployment gates.
What we look for: 5+ years of software testing experience with demonstrated expertise in test automation. 3+
years of testing on the Salesforce platform with a deep understanding of platform limitations. Experience with test
automation frameworks, particularly Playwright. Proficiency in programming languages for test automation.
Experience with CI/CD tools and processes (GitHub Actions, Cumulus CI, MetaDeploy preferred). Performance
testing experience with relevant tools. Experience testing mobile applications using emulators, simulators, or
cloud device farms with automation frameworks. Experience managing test data lifecycles and keeping test
environments synchronized with code deployments through version control systems. Excellent English
communication skills. Self-directed and adaptable. Bonus: Salesforce admin or Blackthorn product experience.
About Blackthorn: Blackthorn turns any organization's Salesforce CRM into event planning software powered by
their connected data. We're a growing, distributed team building Salesforce-native tools that help teams create
events, engage attendees, process payments, and measure success, all without leaving their CRM. Fully remote
global team, stock options for every employee, flexible work week.
