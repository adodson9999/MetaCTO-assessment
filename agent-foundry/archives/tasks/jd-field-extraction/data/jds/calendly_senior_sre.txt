Job Description, Calendly Senior Site Reliability Engineer
Senior Site Reliability Engineer (Calendly, Engineering)
We are looking for a Site Reliability Engineer who will design, build, maintain, and operate our next-generation
infrastructure platform. SREs build their own tools, deploy open-source solutions, and consume cloud services to
extend the platform. You'll empower application engineering teams, evangelize monitoring best practices, and
advise on optimal infrastructure use. Reports to a Senior Manager of Engineering.
On a typical day: Building tools and applications to extend Calendly's infrastructure platform. Evaluating and
deploying cloud native open source tools. Exercising expertise in cloud infrastructure concepts and patterns.
Instituting resilient infrastructure through Infrastructure as Code. Maintaining and improving observability and
offering patterns for application teams. Participating in an on-call rotation. Defining standard practices around
new services, changes, incidents, postmortems, and capacity management.
What we need: An eagerness to learn and mentor others. Creative problem-solving and attention to detail.
Comfort working with internal-facing customers. A strong understanding of Linux. Strong technical knowledge of
cloud infrastructure (especially GCP), distributed systems, and reliability practices. Deep experience designing,
building, and running highly-available production infrastructure. Strong Golang or Python development
experience, especially writing APIs to build and orchestrate cloud infrastructure. Solid knowledge of Kubernetes
patterns (Controllers and Operators). Robust knowledge of networking and cloud networking. Extensive
experience with monitoring tools (especially Datadog). US work authorization required (no sponsorship).
Salary: Tier 1 $237,630 to $287,952; Tier 2 $217,828 to $263,956; Tier 3 $198,025 to $239,960 USD, plus bonus
and equity.
